About

I am a Postdoctoral Informatics Scientist at the Structural Genomics Consortium, University of Oxford. In collaboration with the knowledge exchange network UK SPINE and Open Targets at EMBL-EBI, we aim to identify potential drug targets for age-related morbidities.

Background

2020 - present

Identifying potential novel targets for age-related multimorbidities to drive new understanding of the ageing process and facilitate drug development.
